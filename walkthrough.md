## How to Import to Google Sheets
1.  Open your Google Sheet.
2.  Go to the **"Posts"** sheet.
3.  **File** > **Import**.
4.  Select the **Upload** tab and drag `blog_posts.csv` into the box.
5.  **Import location**: Select "Replace current sheet" or "Append to current sheet".
6.  **Separator type**: Detect automatically.
7.  Click **Import data**.
